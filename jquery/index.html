Chico

This is a photo of Vaquera with her boyfriend, Chico, when they were both less than a year old, before they had a big family of 11 puppies. Chico is an Australian Cattle Dog. Vaquera is a Blue Heeler. Both breeds were developed in the 1800s on a cattle ranch in Australia. They combine Australian and English herding dog breeds along with Dingo.

While Austrialian Cattle Dogs always have a tail - and speckled coats related to their Dalmatian heritage - Blue heelers often are tail-less, a trait inherited from their Scottish Collie heritage. They also have the habit of nipping at the heels of cattle and sheep, which gave them their "heeler" nickname.
